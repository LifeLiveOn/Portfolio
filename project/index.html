About Me

Hello everyone! I'm Thanh, but you can call me Chase. My journey in programming began in 2019, and it's been an exciting and fulfilling adventure ever since.
 My fascination with computers started when I was just 7 years old. I was always intrigued by how everything functioned seamlessly inside that little box. Over time, I realized that it was the result of teamwork and brilliant minds that brought these incredible devices into our daily lives.
 Since the 19th century, computer software has evolved rapidly, and with the advent of AI, creating impactful applications has become more feasible than ever. Initially, my love for gaming overshadowed my interest in programming, but as I embarked on more coding projects, I discovered a deeper connection to my work, realizing the meaningful impact it could have.
 In 2022, I decided to study abroad in the US to further enhance my skills and broaden my horizons. I enjoy diving into analyses, understanding how different applications work, and continuously learning new technologies. Despite the ever-changing market landscape, my passion for programming remains unwavering, and I'm excited about the possibilities that lie ahead.
 Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope you enjoy exploring my stories and projects!
